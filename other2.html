# General Rules

This section covers the rules specific to our Discord server and Minecraft Server. Below is a comprehensive list of prohibited actions. Failure to comply with these rules may result in disciplinary action.

1. Duplicate Messages

Sending duplicate or spam messages over and over disrupts conversations and is not permitted. Violations may result in temporary or permanent restrictions.

2. Extreme Toxicity

Excessive toxicity towards other users ruins the experience for those affected, thus resulting in temporary or even permanent restrictions for offenders. Reports for extreme toxicity may be issued in our Discord Server .

The following categories which fall under extreme toxicity are listed below.

Categories:

  • Harassment
  • Racism
  • Extreme Profanity
  • Usage of Slurs
  • Discriminatory behavior


Punishment Durations:

  • The standard punishment for extreme toxicity is a 7-day mute. The duration doubles with each repeated offense.
  • Any attempt to evade this punishment will result in a significantly increased ban duration.


3. Trolling

Disruptive and annoying actions, such as spam-pinging staff and other users, opening tickets for no reason, and other things along those lines, waste our and other peoples time, and are thus not permitted. Offenses may result in temporary restrictions and bans from opening tickets. Reports for trolling may be issued in our Discord Server .

Punishment Durations:

  • The standard punishment for trolling is a 7-day mute. The duration doubles with each repeated offense.
  • Any attempt to evade this punishment will result in a significantly increased ban duration.


4. Malicious Behaviour

Sharing other users personal information without their consent of any kind, harassing a user, attempting to DDOS a user and anything else with malicious intention is strictly prohibited and will result in an immediate permanent ban from our network.

Reports for Malicious Behaviour may be issued in our Discord Server .

Users caught violating this rule will be issued with a permanent non-appealable blacklist from the entire Network. Our systems will automatically notify a player if they are Blacklisted from the Network.

Behaviour which fall under the network blacklist can be found below this message.

Categories:

  • Revealing phone numbers
  • Revealing full names
  • Revealing addresses
  • Revealing IPs
  • Revealing faces
  • Revealing family members' names
  • Sharing private player information
  • Attempting to DDoS
  • Player harassment
  • Sexual harassment
  • Attempting to use a RAT
  • Defamation attempts
  • Swatting threats or attempts
  • Any malicious intent to harm a player


Punishment Durations:

  • The standard punishment for malicious behavior is a permanent blacklist, from the Network without appeal.


5. Impersonation

Users may not pretend to be others, and offenders of this rule will be asked to adapt their profile to comply with this rule. Ignoring those instructions may result in restrictions later down the line.

Reports for Impersonation may be issued in our Discord Server .

Punishment Durations:

  • The standard punishment for impersonation is a 1-week mute, if the player does not comply with given staff instructions to refrain from attempting to impersonate others. The duration doubles with each repeated offense.
  • Any attempt to evade this punishment will result in a significantly increased ban duration.


6. Advertising

You may not promote any servers, competition to Lunalite or not, as well as any other unrelated content of any kind. Exceptions may only apply towards Media Influencers playing on the server during a livestream or Video otherwise you will recieve a Punishment.

Reports for advertising may be issued in our Discord Server .

Punishment Durations:

  • The standard punishment for advertising is a 7-day mute. The duration may vary depending on the severity of the exploit and will double with each repeated offense.
  • Any attempt to evade this punishment will result in an increased ban duration.


7. Inappropriate Content

Sharing any form of inappropriate content, whether its in text, images, GIFs, or videos, is strictly prohibited. Doing so may result in the deletion of the message and a punishment against the offender.

This includes but is not limited to:

  • Gore
  • Pornography
  • NSFW content
  • Hentai

It also includes any attempts at sexual harassment toward other players on the server.

Reports for inappropriate content may be issued in our Discord Server .

# Gameplay Rules

This section outlines the rules specific to the Minecraft server. Below is a comprehensive list of prohibited actions. Violations may result in penalties.

1. Cheating

The usage of modified clients is strictly prohibited, as it provides an unfair advantage to the player. It's important to remember that both our staff and community strongly oppose cheating and are committed to maintaining fair and competitive gameplay enviroment towards all players. We aim to ensure equal treatment for everyone.

Reports for cheating may be issued in our Discord Server .

Primary Allowed Clients:

  • Lunar Client
  • Feather Client
  • Laby Mod
  • Badlion Client
  • Minecraft Vanilla
  • Modrinth Launcher
  • Any general client that does not provide built-in unfair modifications.


What is considered an unfair advantage is listed below. These client modifications may result in an immediate ban.


Disallowed Client Modifications:

  • Reach
  • Aim Assist
  • KillAura
  • Triggerbot
  • Fly
  • Speed
  • AutoJumpReset
  • Velocity
  • Any general modification which provides an unfair advantage.


An important factor to keep in mind is that, in some cases, we reserve the right to perform a screenshare on you, to ensure fair play and verify player intentions. Our staff may conduct a screenshare if you trigger suspicious alerts, in order to verify the legitimacy of your gameplay.


Screenshare Conduct:

  • Screenshares are typically conducted by experienced and trusted members of our staff team. We highly value player privacy and take every measure to ensure that nothing goes wrong during the process.
  • Refusing a screenshare may result in a punishment, as it prevents staff from verifying the legitimacy of your gameplay environment.
  • A screenshare will only be conducted if the staff team believes there is a valid and suspicious reason to do so.
  • Screensharers are strictly prohibited from engaging in any form of malicious activity, this includes doxxing or the use of remote access trojans with the intention of causing harm towards the player.
  • You are required to have standard screenshare services enabled to ensure the process can be performed effectively and accurately.
  • Stalling or exceeding the given time to perform the screenshare may result in a punishment.


Punishment Durations:

Cheating:

  • The standard punishment for cheating may result in a 30-day ban, and the duration is doubled for each repeated offense.
  • Any attempt to evade this punishment will result in an increased ban duration.


Screenshare Refusal:

  • Similar to bans for cheating, refusing a screenshare will result in a 30-day ban, with the duration doubling for each repeated violation.

2. Fight Interruptions

To maintain fair gameplay, we strictly prohibit players from interrupting any kind of fight, as it can discourage others from continuing to practice on our server. We take this behavior very seriously, as it negatively impacts the experience for everyone on the server.

What can be considered a fight interruption:

  • Intentionally attacking two or more players during a fight session with the goal of disrupting them.
  • Deliberately following players after they change spawns, just to interrupt them again.
  • Purposely killing a player when they are at low health in order to steal the kill.


What can not be considered may be considered a fight interruption:

  • Attempting to attack any player participating within a Clan War or Clan Gank.


Reports for fight interruptions may be issued in our Discord Server .

Exceptions:

  • Exceptions for fight interruptions may be made during official Clan Wars or Clan Ganks.
  • This exception does not apply to players who are not actively participating in the clan war or gank. You are still prohibited from interrupting unrelated players to maintain fairness.

Punishment Durations:

  • The standard punishment for fight interruptions is a 3-day ban. The duration doubles with each repeated offense.
  • Any attempt to evade this punishment will result in a significantly increased ban duration.


3. Statistic Farming

Attempting to gain statistics unfairly just to climb the leaderboards is a serious violation of our rules and may result in a punishment. We are committed to ensuring fair gameplay by strictly prohibiting players from intentionally killing others to farm statistics.

Reports for statistic farming may be issued in our Discord Server .

What can be considered Statistic Farming:

  • Repeatedly killing your alternate account or another player who has no armor.

Exceptions:

  • Exceptions may be made during Clan Wars or Clan Ganks.
  • This exception does not apply to players who are not actively participating in the clan war or gank. You are still prohibited from interrupting unrelated players to maintain fairness.

Punishment Durations:

  • The standard punishment for statistic farming is a 7-day ban. The duration doubles with each repeated offense.
  • Any attempt to evade this punishment will result in a significantly increased ban duration.


4. Exploit Abusing

Attempting to find and abuse exploits or major bugs with the intent to harm the gameplay experience will result in a severe punishment. Such actions can cause significant damage to the server and are taken extremely seriously.

Reports for bugs may be submitted in our Discord Server . We highly encourage all players to report bugs rather than abuse them. In some cases, we may even offer rewards for valuable bug reports.

Punishment Durations:

  • The standard punishment for exploit abuse is a 30-day ban. The duration may vary depending on the severity of the exploit and will double with each repeated offense.
  • Any attempt to evade this punishment will result in an increased ban duration.


5. Punishment Evasion

Attempting to evade a Punishment will lead into an extended Punishment duration, up to 90 Days and may even lead to a ban, in some cases. The duration is doubled on repeated offenses.
